📌 Key rules & notes
🔵
What you enter vs auto-calculated
You enter: Step 1 — panel count, panel type, inverter, battery, battery mounting, roof/mounting system, isolator, MC4. Step 2 — labour lines (scaffold, roof, electrical, DNO/MCS). Step 4 — target margin (%). Everything else (materials breakdown, sell price, package totals, competitor row) updates automatically.
⚡
Brand-specific accessories (auto in Step 3)
Alpha G3 inverters — Alpha CT clamp (10m) is added automatically. SolarEdge SE3680H — Energy Net Comm Hub, revenue grade meter, 100A CT clamp and WiFi antenna are added automatically. SolarEdge Home Battery — Y-cable is added when that battery is selected.
✅
VAT @ 0%
Domestic solar PV installations are zero-rated for VAT (no VAT charged to the homeowner on qualifying installs). Figures in this calculator are shown ex VAT as trade costs; customer prices follow your margin — still consistent with 0% VAT domestic solar.
⚠️
Always get DNO approval before install
Submit the correct grid notification: typically G98 for systems up to 3.68kW inverter capacity (per DNO rules), and G99 where a full application is required for larger or export-limited systems. Confirm with the relevant DNO — do not install without approval where required.
⚠️
Material prices — Greentech / Edmundson (Feb 2026)
Trade material costs are based on Greentech / Edmundson pricing, ex VAT, Feb 2026. If ordering significantly later, refresh panel, inverter and battery costs in the matrix. Prices move with supply and exchange rates.
